About Me :

I was born, raised, and currently live in Winnipeg, Manitoba. While I initially pursued graphic design, my path naturally shifted toward programming; a field I hadn’t originally considered. That shift ultimately led to the creation of this site, and what began as a pivot has turned into a deeply fulfilling journey.

Outside of work, I’m an avid enthusiast of video games, music, anime, and film; anything that offers a chance to unwind and explore creativity in different forms. I also have a strong interest in digital media environments, including media servers and home tech setups. These hobbies not only provide balance but also influence the way I approach user experiences in my development work.

Above all, I have a genuine love for coding; both its challenges and triumphs. The ever-evolving nature of technology ensures there's always something new to learn, making it an ideal space for anyone with a curious mind. It’s this continuous learning that fuels my growth as a developer and keeps me excited about what’s next.
