About Me :

I was born, raised, and currently live in Winnipeg, Manitoba. While my journey began in graphic design, my focus naturally transitioned toward programming; a field that continues to challenge and inspire me daily. That shift ultimately led to the creation of this site and has since grown into a long-term passion built on curiosity, creativity, and problem-solving.

Beyond development, I’m deeply involved in photography and music. Whether capturing live performances or expressing creativity through drumming, I find both art forms complement the precision and rhythm I bring to coding. Each discipline sharpens my sense of timing, attention to detail, and appreciation for well-crafted experiences.

Above all, I’m driven by a genuine love for building and improving things; from efficient, user-focused software to creative projects that push my technical and artistic limits. The ever-changing nature of technology ensures there’s always more to learn, and that ongoing growth is what keeps me motivated and excited for what’s next.
